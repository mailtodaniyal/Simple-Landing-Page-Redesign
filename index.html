Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our review card service.

What happens after I place my order?

After placing your order, you'll immediately receive a confirmation email with your receipt. Within 24-48 hours, you'll receive your welcome package with instructions on how to access your dedicated review website and information about when to expect your custom review cards.

How does the money back guarantee work?

If you're not completely satisfied with your results within the first 30 days, simply contact us by phone, text, or email and we'll issue a full refund - no questions asked. You can cancel anytime after that with no penalties.

How are your review cards different from email requests?

Our physical review cards are more engaging and memorable than digital requests that often get ignored. The cards are designed to capture attention at the point of service when customer satisfaction is highest, leading to much higher response rates than email or text requests.

Can I really be the only business in my category?

Yes! We strictly limit our service to one business per category in each local area. For example, we'll work with only one pizzeria in Columbus, one nail salon in Austin, etc. This ensures you stand out from competitors in your immediate area.

How soon will I see results?

Most businesses start seeing an increase in reviews within 2-3 weeks of implementing the cards. The more consistently you distribute the cards to customers, the faster you'll see results. Many clients report doubling their review volume within the first month.
